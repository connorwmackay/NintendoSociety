DS vs Wii: Which has the best games?

Written By Neil 19/11/2020

Here are a few Nintendo games and the ratings /10 I've given them. Let's find out which console has the best games

DS Lite Games Rating Wii Games Rating
Pokemon Diamond 10/10 Mario Kart Wii 10/10
Pokemon Pearl 8/10 Animal Crossing: Let's Go To The City 4/10
Animal Crossing: Wild World 9.5/10 New Super Mario Bros Wii 10/10
New Super Mario Bros 8.5/10 Super Mario Galaxy 7/10
Big Brain Training 3.5/10 Monster 4x4: World Circuit 0/10

According to my ratings, the console with the best games is the DS, with an average score of 7.9 compared to the games on the Wii which had a measly average of 6.2/10! Sorry Wii fans! Games for the DS are 17% better than games for the Wii. Don't buy a Wii.

My Favourite Nintendo Device

Written By Neil 24/11/2020

I have owned a Nintendo DS Lite and a Nintendo Wii. Which one was my favourite? The Wii was great to play with friends but I'd have to choose the DS because I have many memories of having a great time playing games on it such as Pokemon Diamond & Pearl, and Mario Kart. Will I ever buy another Nintendo Device?

Written By Neil 21/11/2020

Welcome to the Nintendo Society! If you enjoy Nintendo games then this is the society for you. We organise Super Smash Brothers tournaments - playing together and having fun. We also love Pokemon and some of us battle it out with Pokemon cards. We also have general Nintendo gaming sessions, where you can have fun and enjoy playing with the other members. The society also has a members-only Discord where you can chat with your fellow members! If you are interested in our society at all, then you can register your interest by pressing the button below.
